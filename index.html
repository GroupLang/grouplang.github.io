Advancing Mechanism Design and Large Language Models

Our Mission

At Grouplang, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of mechanism design and large language (foundational) models. Our goal is to develop innovative solutions that enhance the understanding and application of these cutting-edge technologies.

Introducing agent.market

We are excited to announce the launch of our first product, agent.market, a marketplace for reward-driven agentic LLMs. This platform enables users to explore, utilize, and contribute to the development of advanced language models that are incentivized to perform specific tasks.

Mechanism Design

Mechanism design is a fundamental aspect of our research. We strive to create efficient and effective mechanisms that align incentives and optimize outcomes in various domains, from economics to artificial intelligence.

Large Language Models

Our team is at the forefront of researching and developing large language (foundational) models. We aim to unlock the full potential of these models, enabling more advanced natural language understanding and generation capabilities.
